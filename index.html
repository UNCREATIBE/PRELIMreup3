About

A 7-member HIPHOP / R&B girl group made up of JURIN, CHISA, HINATA, HARVEY, JURIA, MAYA and COCONA.

With their single ‘Tippy Toes’ they were the first artists to debut from XGALX, a global entertainment production company with a mission to produce artists with a defined world view, creating a culture of bold creativity. XG plans to release their first mini album, “NEW DNA”, on September 27th, 2023.

The group are 'XG', which stands for 'Xtraordinary Girls'. With their fresh, inventive music and performance, XG aims to empower young people from all over the world - from all walks of life.
